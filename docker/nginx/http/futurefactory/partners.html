Wall Of Fame

We do not only share knowledge between the teams in the future factory. We also have partnerships with serveral companies. With their support we hope to be able to inspire even more people. By sharing knowledge with our partners we hope to be able to innovate in new ways.

We are proud of our partners and therefore they get a special place on our site and on a wall in the Future Factory!
