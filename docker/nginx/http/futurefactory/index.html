The Future Factory houses five innovative student teams from Twente. From the many different disciplines that the University of Twente and Saxion University of Applied Sciences have to offer, the students work towards a better future. Be it using renewable energy sources, efficient storage or innovative technology, we aim to inspire people to keep innovating.

Inspiration

We aim to inspire a new generation with the fields of technologies that we innovate in.

Innovation

We aim to innovate in renewable energy, robotics, artificial intelligence and the drone industry.

Collaboration

Having mutual goals, stimulates collaboration in all areas of the teams
